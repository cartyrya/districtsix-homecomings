Our Team

Our project is collaborative in nature and connects with various organizations and people. Learn more below!

Team Partners

The District Six Museum
Project Leaders

Established in 1994, the District Six Museum is a memorial of the District Six community in Cape Town, South Africa, and the people forcibly removed. It also serves as a community space.

The South African History Archive (SAHA)
Project Partner

The South African History Archive (SAHA) will lead the review and preparation of oral history interviews. Additionally, SAHA will supply additional oral history interviews related to the history of District Six.

The National Geo-spatial Information (NGI)
Project Partner

The National Geo-spatial Information (NGI), under the Department of Rural Development and Land Reform, will review and prepare the mapping data. Additionally, NGI will supply additional maps relevant for digitally re-constructing the District Six neighborhood.
