Functionality and Technology

Visitors will have access to a georectified 2D map digital map of District Six, which can be manipulated in a similar fashion to google maps. This map will include details about streets and important landmarks to help the visitor orient themselves. Oral histories will be pinned on the map based on the location of the individual's residence or association with a landmark/ location. Visitors can choose a pin and listen to the oral history. Additional photographs can be included with the oral history pins when it enhances the users experience. In addition, landmarks will be accompanied by visuals such as the oral history videos, alternate video such as music, and/or still photographs.
