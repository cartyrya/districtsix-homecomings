Homecomings: A Digital Space for Remembering the People and Places of District Six

This digital project will rebuild the virtual tour aspects of the District Six Museum in Cape Town. Utilizing recently collected map data, we will reconstruct District Six as it was in the 1960s, in respect to its multicultural community, prior to its designation as a “white area” and period of forced removal. Then, using the oral histories the Homecoming Centre has collected, we will impose the oral histories onto the map. Site visitors will be able to interact with the map, selecting which aspects they’d like to engage with, such as oral histories, music, images, and more.

This is meant to serve as an extension of the Homecoming Centre, to provide a virtual homecoming and digital memorialization, for those who are unable to visit the site in person.
